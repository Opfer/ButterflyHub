Identification

Phanus ecitonorum is very similar to P. vitreus, and is only distinguished by the lack of a small hyaline spot in space 2 on the HW. Other ghost-skippers differ in having and extremely irregular row of marginal spots on the HW(as in P. grandis) or having the long hyaline spot in the HW cell divided by a dark line.
